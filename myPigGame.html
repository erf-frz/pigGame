1. A player rolls the dices and his current score will be shown in the current box. The more he rolls the dices,a higher score is added to the current box. If the player feels happy with the score, he clicks the hold button to keep it and then it is the next player's turn.
2. A player loses his ENTIRE current score when at least one of the dices is 1. After that, it's the next player's turn.
3. The players can set the winning score, so that they can change the predefined winning score of 100.
